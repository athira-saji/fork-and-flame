About Us

At Fork & Flame, we believe that food is more than just a meal, it's an experience. Founded with a passion for flavor and a love for hospitality, our mission is to bring authentic, delicious Indian cuisine to your plate using the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes with a modern twist.

Whether you're here for a hearty breakfast, a fulfilling lunch, or a comforting dinner, we’ve crafted our menu to suit every mood and moment. Come for the food, stay for the warmth—and leave with memories that linger like the taste of your favorite dish.
